# Source Brief: Atlas AI Workspace Q2 Review

## Company Context

Atlas AI Workspace sells an AI-enabled internal productivity workspace to mid-market and enterprise teams.

## Quarter Summary

- quarterly recurring revenue grew from $2.6M to $3.1M
- net revenue retention reached 108%
- gross logo retention slipped from 94% to 91%
- enterprise pipeline grew 24% quarter over quarter

## Product Signals

- search-based workflows are the most frequently used feature set
- workflow automation adoption improved after a simplified onboarding checklist
- team-based knowledge spaces improved weekly active usage in larger accounts
- slide export and executive-summary workflows remain underused

## Risks

- onboarding still takes too long for non-technical admins
- some pilots stall because ROI is not obvious to executive sponsors
- usage is concentrated in a small number of power users inside each account

## Leadership Priorities

1. reduce time-to-value in the first 14 days
2. increase multi-user adoption within active accounts
3. improve executive reporting use cases for renewal conversations
