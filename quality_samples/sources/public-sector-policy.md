# Source Brief: Public Sector AI Briefing

## Background

A regional government innovation office is evaluating whether to expand AI assistant use across internal policy teams.

## Current Constraints

- policy teams handle sensitive internal drafts
- procurement requires clear vendor-review checkpoints
- staff capability varies widely across departments
- leadership wants efficiency gains without reducing review rigor

## Evaluation Criteria

1. data handling and auditability
2. ability to work from trusted internal documents
3. ease of adoption for non-technical staff
4. clarity on where human approval remains mandatory

## Open Questions

- which use cases should be piloted first
- what governance guardrails must be in place before expansion
- how to communicate benefits without overselling autonomy
