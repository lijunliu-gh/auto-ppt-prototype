# Source Brief: Internal Enablement Program Outline

## Program Goal

Train account managers to use new AI-assisted account planning workflows consistently.

## Audience

- 120 account managers
- 18 regional leaders
- mixed technical confidence across the team

## Desired Outcomes

- improve consistency of account plans before QBR season
- reduce prep time for renewal and expansion reviews
- help managers explain AI-generated recommendations responsibly

## Constraints

- training time is limited to one 60-minute live session and short follow-up assets
- examples must feel practical, not conceptual
- regional leaders want a simple rollout plan they can repeat locally

## Content Ideas

1. what the workflow changes and what stays human-owned
2. before-and-after examples
3. recommended prompts and common mistakes
4. local rollout checklist for team leads
