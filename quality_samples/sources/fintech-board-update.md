# Source Brief: Fintech Board Update

## Business Snapshot

Northstar Payments provides fraud monitoring and spend controls for finance teams.

## KPI Highlights

- revenue this quarter: $18.4M, up 19% year over year
- adjusted gross margin: 71%, down 2 points due to cloud spend
- enterprise average contract value: up 12%
- sales cycle length: up from 74 days to 89 days
- churn concentrated in smaller self-serve accounts

## Operating Themes

- enterprise segment is outperforming expectations
- support costs increased after a major platform migration
- fraud-alert accuracy improved but false-positive complaints remain visible
- investors are asking for a clearer path to durable margin expansion

## Board Questions

1. is growth quality improving or just top-line volume
2. what is driving slower deal cycles
3. where should the company prioritize efficiency investments over the next two quarters
