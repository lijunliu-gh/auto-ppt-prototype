# Product Brief

Product: Atlas AI Workspace

## Positioning

Atlas AI Workspace is an enterprise productivity product that combines search, drafting, and workflow automation in one interface.

## Target Users

- operations teams
- customer success teams
- internal strategy teams

## Core Value Proposition

- reduce time spent switching across tools
- improve response quality with reusable knowledge
- standardize workflows across teams

## Strategic Priorities

1. improve activation in the first 14 days
2. increase weekly active usage in core workflows
3. convert pilot customers into annual contracts

## Risks

- inconsistent answer quality across scenarios
- weak visibility into ROI for executive buyers
- onboarding friction for non-technical teams
